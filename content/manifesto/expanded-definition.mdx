The seed to a pretty perfect society. The Grid is a designated space of land that a community of people live within, upheld by advanced sustainable technologies to provide humans with a high standard of self-sufficient living.

Grids are designed with specific standards in mind, to boost the quality of the earth and of the human life lived on it. But they are **entirely customisable** in reflection of their communities. People sign up to communities they want to live with, follow their shared ways of life, and are uninterrupted by overseers: no governments, no businesses, no gangsters, no Karen down the road.

There must, of course, be a few overarching, common-sense rules that all Grids follow and that everyone polices, or no society can exist in peace. Those rules form the [Basic Law](/manifesto/basic-law). The thin non-negotiable floor beneath every Grid.

Above that floor, the Grid is yours.
