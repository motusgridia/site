To simplistically lay out a societal system of the near-future that most people can get behind — and in the process, guide all people to a peaceful path in the midst of disaster.

One day, I dream we see the first Grid built on this planet or another, with everyone in awe of its existence and wanting to live within one. I want the desire to be so strong that that first Grid is just the start. Other Grids will branch off it. Eventually, there will be a network of Grids covering an entire planet. We all will have a chance to live in one. This is the goal, the drive, and the great reset for a new society; **a society fixated on growing The Grid Network of connection, unity & harmony.**
