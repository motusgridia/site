The site's **manifesto thread** is:

1. Tradition is failing. Humans only advance by "alien" leaps.
2. Protest without creativity is impotent. We need new systems to offer, not just things to refuse.
3. Those systems must respect individual choice above all. That's [Optionism](/manifesto/optionism).
4. A politically viable bridge from today's state exists. That's the Vargas model.
5. What we're reacting *against* is concentration of power under monopolistic conservatism.
6. What we're building is **Grid**.
